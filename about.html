About Us

Our founder, Dr. Beverly Chiodo, became interested in alternative medicine after she was diagnosed with cancer. Once she began experiencing the dramatic changes in her health, she knew she had to share her knowledge with others.

To raise the healthiest beef in the world on her ranch, Dr. Chiodo began looking for alternatives to traditional feed. So, she began feeding her akaushi cattle hydroponically grown wheatgrass!

Dr. Chiodo partnered with Enactus, a free enterprise organization from Texas State University to help her with her cattle. Enactus soon found their place maintaining Dr. Chiodo's wheatgrass operation. In 2015, this project developed into its own business, Wholesome Habits.

Currently, Dr. Chiodo and Texas State Enactus work together to provide wheatgrass to businesses just like yours. Help us improve the health of others!
