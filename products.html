Our Product

Our wheatgrass is produced using efficient hydroponics. Due to the use of hydroponics, we are able to have complete control over the nutrient balance in our indoor space - which means we can ensure the high quality of our wheatgrass year round for you!

Additionally, we use as little as 2/3rds less water than traditional gardening. Hydroponics saves room, water, soil, and maintenance costs which are higher when ventilating and growing in soil.  The best part? We take these savings and pass them onto you!

Our product is cut fresh daily before being delivered to our customers. We believe fresh wheatgrass is the better tasting and better for you.  Ask us about delivering to your location!
